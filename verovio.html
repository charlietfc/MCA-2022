These are some differences between the musicxml and MEI files' code.

MEI uses attributes far more than musicxml - information on octaves and accidentals is listed on the same line as the note whereas in music xml they are on different lines within the note tag.

musicxml is generally more readable - each section of the score is clearly noted and has its own tag. MEI seems far more computer generated and contains more computer language

the labelling is different - for articulations for example a tent symbol, in MEI, has an xml.id and then a special name 'ten'. In musicxml there is just an articulation tag and a tenuto tag
